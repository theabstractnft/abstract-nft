Hello, we are Abstract NFT

Abstract NFT is a limited edition collection of digital art created from elements of design and ideas with endless limits and possibilities. By owning a Abstract NFT you will be an exclusive member who will be eligible for giveaways, voting rights for suggesting new collection themes, future airdrops, collaboration and many surprise.

Abstract NFT is built on an idea of making something possible that has been one's dream "Owning a NFT collection". Abstract NFT creates artwork with unexplored themes. What was just an idea in mind is a vision for us to build. Through the Abstract NFT initiative, we hope to bring NFT one step closer to collectors by bringing the art to the world through the blockchain path.

"Abstract art is not the creation of another reality but the true vision of reality" -- Piet Mondrian
